About Kokraf

Last updated: January 2026

Our Mission

Kokraf is a browser-based 3D modeling platform designed for creativity without limits. Our mission is to make 3D modeling accessible, collaborative, and seamless for artists, designers, developers, and hobbyists alike — all without requiring complex software installations.

We believe the web application ecosystem provides the most connected and interactive experience for users, enabling real-time collaboration, instant access, and cross-platform flexibility.

What We Offer

  • Free Plan: Full modeling tools, save scenes locally, and 20 exports per month.
  • Pro Plan: Unlimited exports and access to upcoming premium features.

Our Journey

Kokraf is built step by step to combine cutting-edge web technologies with the needs of modern creators:

  1. Phase 1: Real-time 3D scene viewer with interactive objects using Three.js.
  2. Phase 2: Basic modeling tools, account sign up and subscription plan.
  3. Phase 3: Product launch with AI features, and scaling improvements.
  4. Phase 4: Backend and storage for persistent scenes, and sharing.
  5. Phase 5: Real-time multiplayer collaboration with WebSockets and CRDT-based sync.
