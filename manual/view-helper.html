View Helper

The View Helper is a small interactive widget located at the bottom-right corner of the viewport. It visualizes the orientation of the current camera in the scene and helps you quickly navigate to standard views.

Features

  • Displays the X, Y, and Z axes with labeled positive and negative directions.
  • Clicking on an axis animates the camera to align with that direction.
  • Supports smooth camera animation while keeping the focus on the current target.
  • Updates dynamically to reflect the camera's current orientation.

Usage

  • Look at the bottom-right corner of the viewport to see the View Helper.
  • Click on any labeled axis (X, Y, or Z) to rotate the camera to that view.
  • The opacity of the helper adjusts automatically to show which axes are in front of the camera.
