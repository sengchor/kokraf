Camera View

The Camera View allows you to quickly switch between different cameras in your scene, including the default editor camera and any custom cameras you add.

Location

  • The camera dropdown is located at the top-right corner of the viewport.

How It Works

  • By default, the viewport uses the editor's main camera.
  • When you add a new camera to the scene, it will automatically appear in the camera selection list.
  • Selecting a camera from the dropdown will switch the viewport to that camera view.
  • The default camera is labeled CAMERA in the dropdown, and other cameras are labeled by type (e.g., Perspective, Orthographic).
  • Switching cameras does not change the scene; it only changes the viewport perspective.

Notes

  • You can add multiple cameras to your scene for different views or cinematic setups.
  • The camera dropdown updates automatically when cameras are added or removed from the scene.

Using multiple cameras is useful for previewing the scene from different angles, setting up cinematic shots, or testing lighting and composition.
