Extrude Tool

The Extrude Tool allows you to create new geometry by extending selected vertices, edges, or faces in Edit Mode.

You can control extrusion visually using the Transform Gizmo.

Extrusion is essential for modeling complex shapes. New geometry is connected to the original selection.

How to Use Extrude

  • Select the vertices, edges, or faces you want to extrude.
  • Activate the Extrude Tool. E
  • Move the mouse to extend the selection along the normal direction.
  • Confirm with Left Click or Enter.
  • Cancel with Esc. The extruded geometry will remain, but new vertices will return to their original position.

Axis Constraints

You can constrain extrusion to a specific axis for precise control.

  • Press X, Y, or Z after activating Extrude to lock the extrusion to that axis.

Transform During Extrude

After extruding, you can immediately move, rotate, or scale the new geometry without deactivating the tool.

  • Move the mouse to position the extrusion.
  • Use G, R, or S to transform the extrusion in real-time.

Extrusion Direction

  • With at least one face selected: The extrusion direction follows the average normal of the selected faces.
  • No faces selected: The extrusion moves freely in any direction based on mouse movement.

Extrude works on all selection types (vertex, edge, face), but the resulting geometry depends on what is selected.
