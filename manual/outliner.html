Outliner

The Outliner is located in the sidebar and displays a hierarchical list of all objects in your scene. It provides a visual representation of your scene graph and allows you to select, organize, and reorder objects efficiently.

Features

  • Object Selection: Click on any object in the Outliner to select it. The selected object is highlighted both in the Outliner and in the viewport.
  • Hierarchy View: Child objects are displayed nested under their parent objects, reflecting the structure of your scene.
  • Drag & Drop Reordering: You can drag objects to reorder or reparent them:
    • Drag Above/Below: Move the object before or after another object at the same hierarchy level.
    • Drag Into: Make the object a child of another object.
  • Automatic Updates: The Outliner automatically refreshes when objects are added, removed, renamed, or moved in the scene hierarchy.

Restrictions

  • Objects cannot be made children of themselves or any of their descendants.
  • Dragging multiple selected objects is disabled to prevent hierarchy conflicts.

Integration with Selection

Selecting an object in the Outliner automatically updates the selection in the viewport and the toolbar. This keeps all selection-based tools synchronized, so you can interact with your objects consistently across the interface.
