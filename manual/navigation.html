Navigation

Navigation in Kokraf lets you move around the 3D scene smoothly using your mouse and keyboard.

Camera Controls

  • Orbit — Rotate the camera around the target. Middle Mouse
  • Pan — Move the camera horizontally and vertically. Shift + Middle Mouse
  • Zoom — Move closer or farther from the scene. Mouse Wheel

View Helper

The View Helper shows the current camera orientation and allows quick snapping to standard views.

  • Click an axis on the view helper to snap the camera to one of the six directions: X (Front), Y (Right), Z (Up), -X (Back), -Y (Left), -Z (Down)
